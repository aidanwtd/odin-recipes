Grilled Cheese


A grilled cheese sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 or more slices of bread
  • Butter
  • Cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Butter one side of a slice of bread, place butter-side down in skillet; add one slice of cheese. Butter second slice on one side and place butter-side up on cheese
  2. Cook until lightly browned on one side, flip and continue cooking until cheese is melted. Repeat however many times needed.
